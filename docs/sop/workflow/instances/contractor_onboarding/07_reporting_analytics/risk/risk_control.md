# Risk & Control – Reporting & Analytics

## Risks
- Inaccurate reports.  
- Delayed updates.  
- Data leakage in shared reports.  

## Controls
- Automated ETL validation.  
- Restricted access for sensitive reports.  
- Audit logging.
