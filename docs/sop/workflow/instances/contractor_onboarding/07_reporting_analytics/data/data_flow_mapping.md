# Data Flow – Reporting & Analytics

## Sources
- ERP: timesheets, invoices, payroll, accounts.  

## Transformations
- Aggregate data.  
- Normalize across contractors and clients.  
- Calculate KPIs.  

## Destinations
- Reporting DB.  
- BI dashboards.  
- Compliance archive.  

## Validation
- Reconcile totals with ERP.
