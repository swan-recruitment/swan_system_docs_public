# Risk & Control – Sales Invoice Generation

## Risks
- Wrong client details.  
- Missing attachment.  
- Duplicate invoice.  
- Incorrect totals.  

## Controls
- Invoice number manager.  
- Validation of totals.  
- Pre-send checklist.
