# Risk & Control – Timesheet & Invoice Entry

## Risks
- Wrong entry (manual error).  
- Missing invoice data.  
- Double entry.  
- Non-compliant rates.  

## Controls
- Validation rules in ERP.  
- Dual verification for high-value invoices.  
- Weekly audit logs.
