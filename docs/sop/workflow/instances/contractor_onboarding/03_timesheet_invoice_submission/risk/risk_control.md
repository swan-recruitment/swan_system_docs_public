# Risk & Control – Timesheet & Invoice Submission

## Risks
- Late submission.  
- Incorrect hours or invoice mismatch.  
- Missing invoices.  
- Duplicate files.  

## Controls
- Submission deadline rules.  
- Validation scripts.  
- Duplicate detection.  
- Manual approval/review queue.
