# Risk & Control – Payroll & Accounts

## Risks
- Wrong bank details.  
- Duplicate payment.  
- Incorrect rate applied.  
- Late payment.  

## Controls
- Bank validation rules.  
- Dual-approval of payroll runs.  
- Reconciliation checks.  
- Automated alerts for errors.
